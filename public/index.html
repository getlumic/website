The problem

The work that runs your business is burying the people running it.

Your apps already have your data. Your team is already doing the work. But the spreadsheet pulls, the manual report-building, the inbox that has to be processed by hand, the same monthly numbers chased down across three systems — somewhere along the way, the running-the-business part started getting buried under the office-work part. You don't need another SaaS subscription. You don't need to hire an $80K analyst to clean up board reports. You need someone to build the system that does it for you — and stay on to keep it running.

Reports take hours to build. Pivot tables, exports, copy-paste, manual stitching — every Monday morning, every month-end.
Email is eating your day. Invoices, payroll, client status updates, document workflows — the inbox has become a full-time job for someone on your team.
Enterprise BI doesn't fit. Tableau, Power BI, Snowflake — six-figure stacks built for companies with data teams. You don't have one.
Excel is everyone's dashboard. Stale by Tuesday. Different numbers in every department. No one trusts the totals.

"Honestly, this is better than the BI tools I've used at much larger companies."

Bradford, R. — VP of Sales, $20M manufacturer
What it does

Connect. Visualize. Automate. Share.

Lumic isn't a new tool to learn — it's the layer that ties together the tools you already use.

Connect

Read directly from the apps and inboxes you already use — CRM, billing, scheduling, accounting, email. Read-only. No migration. No "data warehouse" project.

  • Works with whatever you run today
  • Cloud apps, on-prem databases, inboxes, spreadsheets
  • One adapter per source — built once

Visualize

Beautiful, fast dashboards built around the questions an owner actually asks. Drill down in two clicks. Filter without writing a query.

  • Live KPIs & trend charts
  • Multi-level drill-downs
  • Light and dark, every screen size

Automate

AI agents that handle the repetitive work that used to eat someone's day. Invoices extracted from emails, payroll intake processed, status updates sent, document workflows running on their own. They do the work — you approve the result in 30 seconds, not 3 hours.

  • Inbox-driven AI agents
  • Document extraction & processing
  • One-tap approval workflows

Share

One login for your whole team. Same beautiful view on phone, tablet, laptop. Branded for your business — your name, your colors, your domain.

  • Role-based access
  • Mobile-first dashboards
  • Custom domain & theme

Why lumic
